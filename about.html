About

Small software, built with care.

Brixel House is the app and game brand of Mario Gravina and invited collaborators.

Brixel House builds focused apps, wholesome games, and thoughtful tools for iPhone, Apple Watch, macOS, Windows, and related platforms. Some projects are practical. Some are experimental. Some are just meant to be playful.

The common thread is care: software should respect the user’s time, privacy, and attention. Brixel House projects avoid ads, tracking schemes, engagement traps, and pay-to-progress mechanics.

What we make

  • Focused utility apps that do one job clearly.
  • Small games built around fair rules and approachable play.
  • Desktop and simulator tools for practical workflows.
  • Local-first experiments where privacy and traceability matter.

What we avoid

  • Ads and behavioral tracking.
  • Manipulative streaks, fake urgency, and dark-pattern design.
  • Pay-to-progress mechanics and gambling-like reward loops.
  • Unnecessary accounts or data collection.
