Privacy

Privacy-first by default.

Brixel House projects are designed to avoid unnecessary data collection.

Brixel House does not design apps around advertising profiles, behavioral tracking, or unnecessary account systems.

Data collection

Brixel House apps should collect as little user data as possible. When an app does not need personal information to function, it should not ask for it.

Advertising and tracking

Brixel House apps do not use third-party advertising networks or tracking systems for behavioral advertising.

Notifications

Some apps may use local notifications, such as reminders for upcoming events. These notifications are handled by the device operating system and are used only for app functionality.

Future apps

Privacy details may vary by app. Each released app should include its own privacy information when needed.
