Football Schedule Alerts 2026

The app uses local notifications to remind users about upcoming matches. Notification delivery depends on device notification permissions, Focus settings, and iOS notification behavior.

Common checks

  • Confirm notifications are allowed in iOS Settings.
  • Confirm the app is not blocked by Focus or Do Not Disturb settings.
  • Open the app and refresh upcoming reminders if match data has changed.
  • Confirm the device date, time, and time zone are correct.

Contact

For support, use the contact method listed with the app or on the App Store support page.
